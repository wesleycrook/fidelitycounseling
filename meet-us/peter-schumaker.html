Peter Schumaker, LPC

As a father of three children, and a husband of 20+ years, I understand many of the challenges that family life presents. I have seen successful professional men and women feel defeated by the struggles they face at home. I have had the opportunity to work with individuals as they change painful patterns and find joy they previously thought impossible. It sometimes is a long, difficult journey; but it does not have to be traveled alone. I enjoy working with whole families, or seeing individual members of the family to help them find a new way of living together.
