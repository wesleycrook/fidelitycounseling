Donna Tilley, LPC

As a wife of 40+ years, a mom, a grandmother and a myriad of other common titles I have numerous life experiences. These have taught me a great deal about dealing with conflict and resolving issues. I enjoy helping others consider how they might overcome obstacles and develop relationships that are meaningful. Whether you are dealing with a difficult marriage, personal identity struggles or are in the throes of grief there is help. We can work together to build a better you.

My husband and I have served in pastoral ministry for 35+ years. Pastoral counseling has given me the pleasure of helping individuals, couples and families work toward becoming all God has designed them to be. God desires for each person to be whole in mind, body and soul.

I have a special interest in working with couples and families to build relationships that are healthy and enjoyable. I also have a passion for those who have suffered loss and are grieving. If you are desirous of becoming a better you I welcome the opportunity to work with you.
